February 14: Army Black Day – A Day of Remembrance 🖤

February 14 is not just about Valentine’s Day in India; it is also observed as Army Black Day in remembrance of the Pulwama attack that took place on February 14, 2019. This tragic event remains one of the deadliest attacks on Indian security forces, leaving the nation in shock and mourning.

🔹 What Happened on February 14, 2019? On this day, a suicide bomber targeted a CRPF (Central Reserve Police Force) convoy in Pulwama, Jammu & Kashmir. The attack resulted in the death of 40 brave soldiers, making it one of the worst terrorist attacks in India's history. The Jaish-e-Mohammed (JeM) terrorist group claimed responsibility for the attack. In response, India carried out the Balakot airstrike on February 26, 2019, targeting terrorist camps across the border.
